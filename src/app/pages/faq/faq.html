Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about ordering, shipping, returns, and account management.

How do I place an order? ▼
Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. Enter shipping details, choose a payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation email with the order number.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and supported digital wallets. Payment options available at checkout may vary by region.
How long does shipping take and how much does it cost? ▼
Shipping times depend on the selected service and destination. Standard shipping typically takes 3–7 business days. Exact costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and address; free shipping promotions may apply.
How can I track my order? ▼
After your order ships, we email a tracking number and link. You can also view tracking in your account under "Orders" using your order number.
Can I change or cancel my order? ▼
You can request changes or cancellations within a short window after placing the order. Visit your `Orders` page or contact support with your order number; we'll do our best to help before the order ships.
What is your returns and refund policy? ▼
If you're not satisfied, return eligible items within 30 days in their original condition. Refunds are issued to the original payment method after we receive the item. See the Returns section in your account for start-a-return instructions.
Do I need an account to place an order? ▼
You can checkout as a guest or create an account to speed up future purchases, save addresses, and view order history.
How do you protect my payment and personal information? ▼
We use industry-standard encryption for payments and follow strict data privacy practices. For details, review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
How can I contact customer support? ▼
Visit the Contact page or email support at support@example.com with your order number. Support hours and live chat availability are listed on the Contact page.
